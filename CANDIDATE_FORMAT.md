# Candidate Discovery Format

Each discovery candidate must be stored as a markdown file.

Filename format:

candidate-<short-name>.md

---

## Required Fields

### Title

Short descriptive name.

### Domain

Where the candidate belongs.

Example:

- physics
- chemistry
- mathematics
- information systems

---

### Observation

What pattern suggested the candidate?

---

### Hypothesis

What is believed to exist or be missing?

---

### Supporting Evidence

Patterns, analogies, or structural arguments.

---

### Confidence Level

Low  
Medium  
High

---

### Status

candidate  
under review  
accepted  
rejected  
archived
