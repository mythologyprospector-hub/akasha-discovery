# Candidate: Transitional Phase Gap

Domain: physics

Status: candidate

---

## Observation

Many classification systems in physics describe phases of matter with
sharp transitions between known states.

However, complex systems often exhibit transitional or intermediate
microphases that are not formally classified.

---

## Hypothesis

There may exist transitional microphase states between known phases
that have not yet been formally modeled or categorized.

---

## Supporting Patterns

• phase diagrams often show discontinuities  
• symmetry breaking suggests intermediate states  
• similar structures exist in condensed matter physics
